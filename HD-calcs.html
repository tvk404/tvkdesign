Home Depot

Research and design for Home Depots online calculators.

Role 

• Competitive research
• A/B testing
• Mockups
• Presentation creation
• Presentation delivery

Breakdown

Home Depot was lacking in a user-friendly and cross site synergy with their calculators.

Amount of paint needed, how big of an A/C unit you need, how much hardwood, etc.

My team dug in and looked at top tier competitors to glean from varying industries and markets to troubleshoot and design a site-wide, uniform model that can apply to whatever need the user/consumer would need to expedite their project, bring ease of use and peace of mind.
