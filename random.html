DJ For the Day

At my tenure at Pinnacle Promotions, there was a monthly drawing for "DJ's" for every friday. They'd get control over the office speakers and craft their own Spotify playlist. They'd also get a poster I'd get to design their persona.

It was a great way to get to know my co-workers better and flex some more creative work and break up the mundane.
