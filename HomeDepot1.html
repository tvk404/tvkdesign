Home Depot

Proposed Blue Sky Design of Home Depots Digital Decor showcase.

Roles 

• User Research
• Image Curation/Editing
• Art Direction
• Wireframing
• Web Design

Breakdown

Brainstormed redesign for the Shop by Poom pages under Digital Decor category. Home Depot has a quarterly print catalog that coincides with the digital experience, yet has much more character than the .com version.

I added distinct style nods to fit the character of the sets through tpyograhpy and visual design. This adds to the overall visual aesthetic of the page and breaks the typical mold seen sitewide as the company looks to branch out into the interior decor market.

With breaking up the grid it allowed us to showcase our photography assets better. This design solves some unavoidable painpoints due to limitations of Home Depot’s propreitary CMS that is used to build these pages. Changing the layout of the room style and the “style tags,” It allowed us to make every descriptor an individual link. This will eventually allow users to search by styletag once the meta data catches up to the design capabilities.
