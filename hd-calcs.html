Home Depot Calculators

The calculators site-wide varied in style and being user friendly. We set out to change that

Roles:

• Competitive Research
• Visual Design
• Wireframing
• Presentation creation

Breakdown:

Visitors to the site were voicing frustrations with the different calculators to help with the amount of materials they'd need for a project. Paint was different than drywall which was different than flooring.

While all had a similar reason for use, we found competitors in the Paint and flooring industries had some of the most effective and easy to use so we got to work listening to the people.
