FiveBox

UI Design for their partnership with GA Power + Recleim appliance pickup wizard

Role 

• UX Ideation
• Wireframes
• Image Asset Creation
• UI Design

Breakdown

FiveBox approach me to reskin their applince pickup wizard. The developers had everything good to go, but being the smart guys that they are, they realized they were missing an aesthetic interface.

Georgia Power partnered with Recleim to help owners recycle their used or unwanted appliance in an earth-friendly manner.

Taking visual cues and styles from Recleims site, I recreated their character to make it a seamless experience on mobile, tablet and desktop viewports.
