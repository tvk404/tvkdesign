Role

Breakdown

AT&T had 2 completely unrelated visual standards between their customer facing branding and their Enterprise Business platforms.

This project was to streamline the visual styles and branding for the Enterprise branch with that of the image of AT&T.

Taking the familiarity and ease of use from the customer-facing billing pages, we effectively re-created the full gamut of all necessary pages with the updated styles and code snippets to streamline design and expedite product launches.
