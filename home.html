HELLO.

I'm Tim, a multi-faceted UX/Interaction Designer who loves 
creating exceptional experiences.

To make it smart. To make it function. To make it work. To make it pretty.

I live in Atlanta Oakland.


With a broad brush of skills ranging from Graphic Design, UX research and UI design, Interaction design, motion and animation, web design and front-end coding, branding and identity, art direction, photography and mentoring, I have a strong grasp of what effective visual design on all levels of ideation brings to a great experience. I enjoy all aspects of "designing" something.

With over 10 years of experience and a range of competencies, I continue to stay on top of my game to be a well-rounded creative/techy/problem solver. 

I keep up daily with trends, fads or gamechangers, and technologies to stay up-to-date on the constantly changing landscape.

No matter the project at hand, intelligent, creative and stimulating user-centric design is my passion.
