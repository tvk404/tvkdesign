DJ for the Day

Visual design for co-workers "DJ for the Day" posters.

Role 

• Visual Design
• Conversation

Breakdown

While at Pinnacle Promotions, we'd have a company-wide drawing for a DJ for the Day where the winner would get to play their curated Spotify playlist across the company sound system.

One of the perks was a custom 11x17 poster of their DJ persona they'd get to keep as well as have one framed for our social area.

In the end, it was a great way to get to know my co-workers better, try some new design ideas and get to run with some pretty fun and random ideas.
